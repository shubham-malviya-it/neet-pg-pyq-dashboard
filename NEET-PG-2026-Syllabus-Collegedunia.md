NEET PG Syllabus 2026: What’s New?

## NEET PG Syllabus 2026: What’s New?

NBEMS has introduced structural changes to NEET PG 2026. **The total questions drop from 200 to 180, reducing the maximum marks from 800 to 720.** A section-wise time-bound format is also new for 2026. Once a section ends, you cannot return to it. These changes are officially notified in the Information Bulletin released on July 1, 2026. The topic-wise syllabus content itself remains stable for 2026.
